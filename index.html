About Me

Thanks for stopping by! My name is Vidya, and I graduated from UC San Diego in 2019 with a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics & Computer Science. I began my Master's in Computer Science (also at UCSD!) in Fall 2020, and am specializing in machine learning and human-computer interaction. At UCSD, I've been involved in organizations and initiatives like The Design Lab, CS Foreach, and Design for America.

I've had the opportunity to intern at some awesome startups (Ginkgo Bioworks and Strava) and larger companies like Zoom, where I was recently a product management intern. I hope to continue working on products and ideas that make a positive impact on the world and make people happier. For more information about the projects I've worked on, see my LinkedIn and the Work tab on this website.

Outside of work and school, I enjoy reading, cooking, crossword puzzles, and hikes.
